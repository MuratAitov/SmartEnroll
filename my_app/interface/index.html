Final Examination Information

Final examinations are held at the end of each semester as indicated in the University calendar. Students are responsible for consulting the final examination schedule before making travel arrangements.

Final examinations or their equivalent can be administered only during the final examination period. Students are not expected to take more than two final examinations in one calendar day.
